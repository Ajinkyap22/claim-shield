Track: 

The Healthcare Claim Shield (Healthcare)

Autonomous Healthcare Compliance & Revenue Assurance


The Mission:
The healthcare system loses billions to claim denials caused by simple documentation errors. Your task is to build a 'Compliance Shield' — an agent that acts as a firewall between the doctor and the insurer.


Operational Blueprint:
A sophisticated orchestrator that processes raw doctor dictations (audio/text) and standardizes them into valid HL7/FHIR formats. The agent must cross-reference these notes against a medical Data Set and, crucially, read complex Insurance Policy PDFs to verify coverage before a claim is ever submitted.
